## Goal

Add clear accessible names to remaining topbar/key-pool utility controls that only had short visible text (`退出登录`, `测试 Webhook`, `刷新状态`, `批量导入`).

## Requirements

- aria-label on `#logout`, `#testWebhook`, `#refresh`, `#bulkImportBtn`.
- Keep short visible labels for density.
- Unit pins; verify green.
